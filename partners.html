Tuwaiq Academy has numerous partnerships with major global technology organizations. Virtual reality-augmented reality Application development Software development

Meta's first academy in the Middle East and North Africa, dedicated to developing and qualifying national capabilities in the fields of artificial intelligence and virtual worlds.
